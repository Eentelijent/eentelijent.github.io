Chemical bonding

In an element/compound, forces hold the particles together. The forces are called chemical bonds

Types of chemical bonding

  • Ionic – metal + non-metal
  • Covalent – non-metal + non-metal
  • Metallic – metal + metal

Valence electrons

Metals — usually 1, 2, or 3 → give away electrons
Non-metals — usually 5, 6, or 7 → gain electrons
