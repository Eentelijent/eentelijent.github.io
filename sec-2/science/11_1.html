Chemical Changes

Introduction
  1. Changes can be classified as either physical or chemical
  2. Differences between physical changes and chemical changes:
    Physical Chemical
    No new substances formed
    		 New substances are formed
    Reversible
    		 Irreversible

  3. In a chemical reaction, energy is either taken in or taken out
