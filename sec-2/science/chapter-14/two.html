Digestive System

Stomach and Accessory Organs

Stomach
The stomach is a bag-like region with gastric glands that produces acids and enzymes
  • Acid:
    • kills bacteria and parasites
    • provides a suitable environment for the enzymes
  • Enzymes
    • Protease
    • Speeds up the breakdown of proteins into polypeptides
  • Food that leaves the stomach is called chyme

Accessory organs

Accessory are not part of, but are important to, the digestive system.
  1. Liver
  2. Gall bladder
  3. Pancreas
Liver
  • Produces bile

Gall bladder
  • Stores bile
  • Bile can solidify and form gall stones

Pancreas
  • Produces pancreatic insulin
  • Produces insulin
