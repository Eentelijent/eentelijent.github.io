Definition: Work done to drive a unit of charge through the component

SI Unit: Volt (V)

  • One volt of p.d. = one joule of work is done to drive a Coulomb through a component

Advantages
  • Batteries used up slower

  • Bulbs can be controlled independently

Disadvantages
  • Bulbs are less bright

  • If one bulb is spoilt, all other bulbs do not light up
  • Shorter battery lifespan
